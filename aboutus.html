About Us

Sunbeam Motor Car Company Limited was a British motor car manufacturer.
With its works at Moorfields in Blakenhall, a suburb of Wolverhampton in Staffordshire, now West Midlands.

Its Sunbeam name had been registered by John Marston in 1888 for his bicycle manufacturing business. Sunbeam motor car manufacture began in 1901. The motor business was sold to a newly incorporated Sunbeam Motor Car Company Limited in 1905 to separate it from Marston's pedal bicycle business; Sunbeam motorcycles were not made until 1912.
